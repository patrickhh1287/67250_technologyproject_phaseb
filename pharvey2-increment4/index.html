Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life.

MonoMuse was founded in 2018 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, with a mission to make technology-driven art accessible to everyone. Since then, the museum has welcomed over 100,000 visitors, partnered with Carnegie Mellon University for an Interactive Arts Residency, and received the National Arts Innovation Award. Our rotating exhibitions feature works by digital artists, AI-driven installations, and immersive audiovisual environments. We also offer educational programming for students, families, and community groups. Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity.
