Our Mission

MonoMuse exists to ignite curiosity at the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. We believe that innovation is most powerful when it is accessible, inclusive, and rooted in culture. Our mission is to create a space where visitors of all backgrounds can engage with ideas that shape our digital future.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse welcomes a diverse community of visitors, including:

  • First-time museum-goers seeking an approachable introduction to contemporary art
  • Students and educators exploring the relationship between technology and creativity
  • Families looking for interactive, hands-on learning experiences
  • Returning members and art enthusiasts discovering rotating collections
  • Researchers and professionals in art, design, and technology fields

Founding Year

MonoMuse was founded in 2018 with the vision of bridging the gap between traditional museum experiences and the rapidly evolving world of digital art and interactive media.

Milestones

  • 2018 — MonoMuse opens its doors to the public in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania
  • 2019 — Launches inaugural exhibition "Digital Horizons: Art Beyond the Screen"
  • 2020 — Introduces virtual exhibition tours, reaching over 50,000 online visitors
  • 2021 — Partners with Carnegie Mellon University for the Interactive Arts Residency
  • 2022 — Surpasses 100,000 in-person visitors; opens a dedicated education wing
  • 2023 — Receives the National Arts Innovation Award for community engagement
  • 2024 — Debuts the immersive "Synth & Soul" installation by artist collective NOVA
  • 2025 — Launches website redesign initiative to improve visitor experience and ticket purchasing
