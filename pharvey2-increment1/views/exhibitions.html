Exhibitions

Synth & Soul: The Sound of Color

Step into an immersive audiovisual environment where sound waves generate real-time digital paintings. Created by artist collective NOVA, this installation translates live music into evolving visual compositions, inviting visitors to explore the emotional connections between what we hear and what we see. On view through June 2026.

Machine Dreams: AI and the Creative Mind

This thought-provoking exhibition examines how artificial intelligence is reshaping artistic expression. Featuring works co-created by human artists and generative AI systems, Machine Dreams challenges visitors to consider questions of authorship, creativity, and collaboration in the age of intelligent machines. On view through August 2026.

Pixel Playground: Interactive Art for All Ages

Designed for families and younger visitors, Pixel Playground is a hands-on gallery where creativity meets technology. Children and adults alike can build digital sculptures, animate hand-drawn characters, and collaborate on a shared pixel mural that grows throughout the exhibition's run. Ongoing.
