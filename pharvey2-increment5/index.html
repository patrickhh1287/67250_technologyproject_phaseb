Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. Through immersive exhibitions, interactive installations, and rotating collections, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art.

Buy Tickets

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a returning member, our exhibitions offer new perspectives on the role of technology in culture and everyday life.

Plan your visit to experience cutting-edge exhibitions, hands-on experiences, and events designed to inspire curiosity and creativity. MonoMuse hosts rotating collections from digital artists around the world, interactive workshops for all ages, and special evening events that bring together art, music, and technology. Our education wing offers guided tours for school groups, and our membership program provides exclusive access to exhibition previews, artist talks, and behind-the-scenes experiences. We believe that technology and creativity belong to everyone, and we are committed to making our museum an inclusive and welcoming space for all.
